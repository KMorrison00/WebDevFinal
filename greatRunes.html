Godricks Great Rune

Held by Godrick the Grafted This great rune stands as a testament to the golden lineage. Descended from the first elden lord Godfrey This great rune bestows upon its wielder a blessing across all stats of 5 points.

Rykards Great Rune

Held by Praetor Rykard this great rune was distorted upon rykard allowing himself to be eaten and combined with the blasphemous serpent. Its blessing is much like Rykard's blasphemy allowing its bearer to absorb the health of fallen enemies.

Radahn's Great Rune

Held by Radahn a man so stout he could hold back the stars from falling from the sky even when his mind and body were destroyed by scarlet rot. His great rune bestows vigor along with stamina and mental strength. Its power allowed radahn to remain strong for so long despite the rot.

Morgotts Great Rune

First of the Omen Twins and secret king of leyndell. Despite his cursed birth Morgott gained great strength and eventually became ruler of Leyndell. His great rune grants vitality beyond any other.

Mohgs Great Rune

Second of the Omen Twins and hidden far beneath the sewer dungeons of Leyndell. By serving the Blood Mother he has tainted his body, soul, and great rune red. This rune grants its bearer the blessing of blood and a phantom great rune upon sucessful invasion of other player's worlds.

Malenia's Great Rune

Daughter to queen Rennala and Radagon, Malenia is cursed with scarlet rot to which she has lost her right arm and feet. Her great rune bestows lifesteal and reduction of healing from crimson tear flasks

Great Rune of the Unborn

Held by Rennala Queen of the Full Moon. After going insane with grief from her husband leaving her for another she began a cycle of rebirth with her scholars. While unable to be wielded by the player Rennala will grant the player the gift of rebirth through this great rune.
