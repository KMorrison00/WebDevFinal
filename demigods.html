Godrick the Grafted

Godrick is the most recent in a long line of descandants from Godfrey, First Elden Lord, also known as the golden lineage. Godrick maintains his power in Stormwind castle and Limgrave by having his subjects be killed and grafted into his increasingly grotesque body.

Praetor Rykard

One of Radagon proginey Rykard sought to devour the gods and remove their influence from the Lands Between. To accomplish this he fed himself to the blasphemous serpent to obtain power to kill a god.

Starscourge Radahn

Child of Radagon and Rennala Radahn fought Malenia to a stand still during the wars following the Shattering. Unfortunately for him Malenia bloomed her scarlet aeonia and forever transformed him and the lands of Caelid into a scarlet rotted mess. Despite his madness he stills holds back the star beasts from invading the lands between.

Morgott The Omen King

Cursed as an omen child from birth along with his twin brother Mohg, Morgott is nevertheless a part of the Golden Lineage and defends both Stormwind and Leyndell from would be invaders.

Mohg Lord of Blood

Cursed as an omen child from birth along with his twin brother Morgott. Mohg hid himself beneath the city of Leyndell with the other cursed omen children and founded a secret empire woshipping the Blood Mother.

Malenia, Blade of Miquella

Daughter to queen Rennala and Radagon, Malenia is cursed with scarlet rot. But it is both a blessing and a curse. When pushed too far in battle the outer god of rot's powers manifest and transform her into the goddess of rot.
