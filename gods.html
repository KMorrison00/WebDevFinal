Queen Marika

Servant of the Greater Will she was the first to bring chaos to the Land between by shattering the Elden Ring. As punishment the greater will imprisoned her at the center of the Greater Erdtree

Radagon of the Golden Order

Servant of the Greater Will, "other half" to Queen Marika, Second Elden Lord, and former husband of Queen Rennala. He desperately seeks to repair the elden ring and exists as a husk of former hero he once was.

Godfrey, First Elden Lord

Originally a tarnished from the Lands Between he sought to unify the people as their champion. However Queen Marika cast him out in favour of Radagon, sending him to wander the Lands between among his fellow tarnished.

Elden Beast

Sent by the greater will as a supervisor and protector to the Lands between it does not interfere directly with most matters. Except of course those who seek to claim the Elden Ring's power for their own. As a higher servant of the Greater will it wields Radagon's body as a sword.
